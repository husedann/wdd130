Why record your Memories?

Memories are a result of events in a person's life that need to be remembered. MyMemories website allows you to record events in a quick, easy-to-use manner. Tired of trying to keep a daily journal? Childhood memories pop into your mind? Do you remember your first kiss? What was your first car? What did your dad do for a living? These events can now be recorded while waiting for your class to start, while eating your lunch, at the park, at school, anywhere! Easy access on your desktop or phone. Start today, record those memories before they are lost forever.
